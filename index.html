About Us

Reap Agro is working to improve the lives of smallholder farmers by providing timely interest-free financing to support and facilitate them, in the form of inputs and easy credit. Overcoming poverty one farmer at a time, we aim to enable them by decreasing cost, improving yield and managing disasters through our advisory and technological channels. Also catering to the issue of right price for the farmers by buying back farmers produce and then selling it to multinationals eliminating the need for middlemen.

What We Do

Profiling & Clustering

The profiled farmers are put into clusters and background checks are ran to ensure their credibility, so that they are liable for each other incase of defaults regarding loans.

Interest Free Financing

Our interest free loans not only consist of easy credit but are also in the form of inputs for the farmers. Enabling them to have healthy produce and improved yield.

Farmer Advisory

Through weekly visits and crop monitoring, guidance and training are provided to the farmers regarding irrigation, fertilizers, pesticides use, soil testing, and other updates.

Crop Purchase

We mitigate the farmers risk by buying back the produce from them at the right price and selling to multinationals eliminating the need for middle men.
